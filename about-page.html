ERIN FORWOOD

Sydney Australia born and raised, with a love of travle and golbal curiosity. Erin is a freelance Designer, Stylist & creative consultant currently based in Australia. 

With 10 years experience in Styling and Design in the fashion industry, both Sydney and NY. Erin lanuched Limited Collective in 2018 to utlise fashion waste in the local area and quickly founnd a way to turn off cuts into classic jackets with unique details. Two capsule collections and a sold out Pop-up store event later, custome jackets can be purchased on LimitedCollective.com. 

While on a trip to Kerela India, Erin created connection with the local people after hearing stories of the destuctive floods in 2018. She has now established a production team that trains and upskills women.
