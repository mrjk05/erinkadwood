Breast Cancer Trials

'Together'

 Erin and Gretel came together for this project as a result of their shared passion for recycling and reusing fashion as an environmental and creative statement. Their design embraces the subject of breast cancer with signature female style, humour, sexuality and practicality.

The design represents the many facets of womanhood, the many roles of women in our lives and and the broad reach of women touched by breast cancer. The garment is made up of an original lab coat, off-cut maroon faux suede, off-cut print by Australian Designer Millie Hall, and locally sourced shop bought white organza.
